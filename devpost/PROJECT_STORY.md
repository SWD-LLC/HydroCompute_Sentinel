# HydroCompute Sentinel: Devpost Project Story

HydroCompute Sentinel is an AI readiness platform for siting large-scale compute while balancing water, energy, agriculture, and ecosystem stability.

## Problem

AI/data center location decisions have traditionally focused on power, tax, land, and fiber. Water resources, food systems, and ecological resilience are often minimized or ignored—creating regional risks if AI buildout overloads carrying capacity.

Water-intensive compute campuses bring challenges like:
- Local water shortages
- Unreviewed thermal discharge effects on aquatic systems
- Agricultural stress
- Intermittent power grid stress
- Public trust in data-driven risk models

## Solution

HydroCompute Sentinel reframes AI infrastructure siting as a carrying-capacity problem. Key features:
- Deterministic infrastructure risk scoring
- Qwen-powered contradiction and claim-risk agent
- Regulator/public/developer report generator
- Simulated field telemetry validator
- Human approval workflow

## Impact
- Supports decisions that avoid unintended consequences for water, energy, and food systems
- Makes risk scoring explainable and transparent
- Provides rapid regulatory/mitigation packet for stakeholders
