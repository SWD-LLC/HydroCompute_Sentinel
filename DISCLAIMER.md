# Disclaimer

HydroCompute: Sentinel is a prototype decision-support system.

It does not provide legal, engineering, environmental, financial, or regulatory advice. Outputs are draft planning artifacts and require review by qualified professionals, including hydrologists, environmental engineers, utility specialists, permitting authorities, and public-sector decision-makers.

The Sentinel QC Water Node Simulator is a simulated telemetry layer for hackathon demonstration. It is not a real hardware specification or certified monitoring system.
